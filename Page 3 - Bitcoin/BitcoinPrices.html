History of Bitcoin Prices

Feb 6th, 2014 → $748.44 → Mt. Gox, one of the largest bitcoin exchanges handling nearly 70% of bitcoin trading, suspended withdrawals citing technical issues. By the end of the month, Mt. Gox had filed for bankruptcy protection in Japan amid reports that 744,000 bitcoins had been stolen.

 

December 11th, 2014 → 348.27 → Microsoft becomes one of the first major tech companies to support the payment of Bitcoin

 

January 21, 2015 → $230.68 → Coinbase gets a Series C Funding at $75 million at a post-money valuation of $490 million

January 2017 → NHK reported the number of online stores accepting bitcoin in Japan had increased 4.6 times over the past year.

BitPay CEO Stephen Pair declared the company's transaction rate grew 3× from January 2016 to February 2017, and explained usage of bitcoin is growing in B2B supply chain payments.

October 12th, 2017 → $5728.55 → Price broke $5,000 for the first time

 

December 15, 2017 → Price reaches $17436.60

 

December 16, 2017 → $19166.98 → Price rose 5% in 24 hours with its value being up 1824% since January 1st, 2017, to reach a new all-time high

 

December 21st, 2017 → $14182.46 → Price lost one third of its value in 24 hours.

 

February 5th, 2018 → $6332.37→ Price dropped by nearly 50% in 16 days

 

November 14th, 2018 → $5524.80 → Bitcoin Bubble pops and bitcoin market cap falls below $100 billion for the first time since October 2017.

 

March 16, 2020 → $4944.70 → Price dropped by 39% third in 24 hours early during the Covid-19 pandemic.

 

July 27th, 2020 → $11187.78 → Price recovered value lost in COVID- related crash

 

November 24th, 2020 → $19045.74 → All-time 3 year high

 

November 30th, 2020 → $19382.36 → All-time high of $19382.36

 

December 15th, 2020 → $19443.48 → Bitcoin reaches all-time high $19443.48

 

Jan 8th, 2021 → $40519.45 → Bitcoin price crossed 40,000 for the first time

 

Jan 21st, 2021 → $30606.18 → Prices briefly fell by 26%

 

March 13, 2021 → $60743.04 → Prices nearly doubled in value within 2 months.
